I'm Jihyeon Han, a Film and Media student at Sungkyunkwan University with a deep passion for the ways moving images shape how we understand the world around us.

My academic and creative work sits at the crossroads of cinema, digital media, and data storytelling — examining how culture flows through screens, both big and small.

Whether analyzing a frame composition or building an interactive dashboard, I believe the best stories are ones that invite the audience to look closer.
